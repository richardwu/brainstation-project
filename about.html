Our Mission

Homelessness in ontario is an issue that has only worsened over the past decade. In 2013, the homeless hub reported that over 10 000 ontarians were homeless and the numbers have only risen since.Being homeless means these people don't have access to 3 of the most essential components to any human's survival: food, shelter, and clothing. With the help of the community, they have a fighting chance to survive, recover, and once again become contributing and harding working members of society.
